About Me

Hello there! My name is Christian and I’m the developer of this site. I’ve recently left Millard North High School whilst graduating with my regular and IB diplomas in pursuit of higher education. Currently, I am attending college in the state of California as a second-year student on the path to a Biomedical Engineering major.

As a freelancing website developer and graphic designer, I spend most of my freed time working on digital portfolios and creating new pieces of art. Within the last couple of years, I have been developing an expertise in graphic design whilst advancing my artistic ability in frontend design. Atypical to my current technological repertoire, I plan on pursuing my humanitarian ideals by providing for those who are in need of immediate care through my future studies in the field of medicine.

Changes To This Website

Over the course of eight redesigns, this being the ninth, I wanted to bring back a couple ideas I had for this website but had yet to learn how to make those styles work. Included in this redesign, I've consolidated three of my larger and more personal subdomains on this webpage for easier access and a more holistic representation of my artistic character. You may notice that my projects subdomain no longer exists as I've decided to create a tab for you listed as "Works". You may have also noticed that this tab is also very short in projects that I have created as I have excluded a lot of personal portfolios. My reasoning for this is that those projects were developed under C&C Studios in which you can find under the studio's project subdomain.
