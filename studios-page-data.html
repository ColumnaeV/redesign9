Co-founding C&C Studios

The first thing you should know being on this page, is that this is NOT the C&C Studios main page. Rather, this is my summary/cover page of my work with the organisation.

To continue, the idea of C&C Studios started out in my junior year of high school as a IB CAS project. The idea of it was an organisation of students learning how to code collectively from each other. After graduating high school and moving out to California, I was working on Camille's photography website where I came across the core idea of C&C Studios again, but modified for both coding and photography. From here, I finished developing her website, but as the subdomain of photography under the organisation's domain.Considering that coding and photography wasn't enough to launch an organisation, I reached out to one of my best mates from high school, Austin, to be a part as a co-founder and Department Head of Editing.

Department Head of Web Development

Deriving from my title, I manage anything related to website development published under the organisation's domain. The publication of a project is easily justifiable for me as long as it follows the core values of the organisation: promoting creativity through artistic inspiration. Even though placed at the highest position of a department, I still continue to work the same as any organsation member of the department in consulting on any web development aspect of a project.
